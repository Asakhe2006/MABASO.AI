Mabaso AI

AI lecture transcription and study guide workspace for students

Mabaso AI helps students turn lecture recordings, notes, slides, and past papers into transcripts, study guides, worked examples, flashcards, quizzes, teacher walkthroughs, podcasts, presentations, and collaboration-ready revision material.

Features Workflow Supported sources Study outputs

Main features

  • Transcribe lecture audio and video into searchable text.
  • Generate structured study guides from transcripts, notes, slides, and past papers.
  • Create worked examples, formula sheets, flashcards, and timed tests.
  • Produce teacher-style explanations, podcasts, and presentation decks for revision.
  • Store study materials in one workspace and collaborate in shared rooms.

How students use Mabaso AI

  1. Upload or record a lecture, then add lecture notes, slides, or past papers.
  2. Let Mabaso AI read the sources, transcribe the lecture, and merge the material into one study context.
  3. Generate a study guide, examples, formulas, quizzes, and teaching support from the same lecture workspace.
  4. Review, download, and revisit saved materials from the My Materials area.

Supported study sources

Mabaso AI is designed for university-style revision workflows and supports lecture recordings, lecture notes, slide decks, past question papers, marking memos, and uploaded reference images that help explain difficult concepts.

Study outputs students can generate

Students can generate adaptive study guides, clearer worked examples, teacher-guided lesson audio, quizzes with marking, presentation slides, collaborative study rooms, and export-ready revision materials from a single lecture topic.
